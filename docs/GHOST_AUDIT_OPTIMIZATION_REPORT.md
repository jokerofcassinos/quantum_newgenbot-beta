## 16. Optimization Recommendations

### 16.1 Priority 1: SELLS ONLY Mode (Highest Impact)

**Action:** Restrict the strategy to SELL trades only.
- **Expected impact:** +$230,366 improvement (from -$65K to +$165K)
- **Rationale:** SELL trades are +$82K net, BUY trades are -$148K net. Eliminating BUY trades removes the single biggest drag on performance.
- **Specific change:** Set `max_trades_per_side` or add a filter: `if direction == "BUY": veto("buy_disabled_for_profitability")`

### 16.2 Priority 2: Minimum Duration Filter

**Action:** Add a minimum expected duration filter or avoid trades that historically resolve quickly.
- **Expected impact:** Avoid $135K in very-short-trade losses and $234K in short-trade losses = **$369K avoided**
- **Rationale:** Trades <=5 bars have 6.6% WR. Trades 6-15 bars have 10.5% WR. Trades >30 bars have 32.6% WR and +$387K profit.
- **Specific change:** Add post-entry monitoring: if trade resolves in <=10 bars, flag for analysis. Pre-entry: avoid setups in high-volatility conditions that tend to resolve quickly.

### 16.3 Priority 4: Hour-Based Session Refinement

**Action:** Instead of blanket weekend veto, implement hour-specific weekend rules.
- **Expected impact:** If weekend hours 08:00-09:00 and 17:00-20:00 UTC were allowed:
  - Hours 08-09: 466 trades, 39.7% avg WR, +$82,533 PnL
  - Hours 17-20: 624 trades, 31.1% avg WR, +$34,844 PnL
  - **Total recoverable: ~$117,377**

### 16.4 Priority 5: Hour-Based Trading Restrictions

**Action:** Disable trading during destructive hours (10:00-16:00 UTC).
- **Expected impact:** These hours collectively lose ~$150K
- **Specific change:** Add time-based veto: `if 10 <= hour_utc <= 16: veto("destructive_hours")`

### 16.5 Priority 6: Confidence System Recalibration

**Action:** The confidence scoring is inversely correlated with performance. Recalibrate or remove.
- **Rationale:** 0.4-0.5 confidence has 24.6% WR vs 0.6-0.7 confidence at 20.0% WR. The system is overconfident on bad trades.
- **Specific change:** Invert confidence weights or rebuild the confidence model with actual win/loss data.

**Action:** Keep as-is. This filter is working correctly.
- 2,691 trades rejected at 23.8% WR, avoided $539K in losses.
- Consider strengthening: lower the quality threshold further.

### 16.7 Priority 8: Individual RSI Advanced Veto Adjustments

**Action:** The 44 "bad veto" RSI reasons are low-sample outliers. Do NOT disable the advanced_veto filter wholesale. Instead:
- For SELL RSI oversold vetos: Consider raising the threshold from <28 to <20 (fewer vetos on extremely oversold conditions which appear to be winners)
